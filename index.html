Astronaut Collector!


You play the role of the inter-galatic miner on a foreign planet, and must gather the appropriate amount of crystals, displayed, each day to make NASA proud.

There are four different crystals below for you to collect! By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount to your space bag.

It's up to you to collect the right amount of crystals to complete your mission! But be careful! The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you collect it! If you collect too many crystals, your space bag will tear and you will lose all of your crystals!

Don't forget each day, the foreign planet changes the value of each crystal! We are counting on you to bring NASA the right amount everyday! !
